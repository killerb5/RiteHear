Privacy Summary

Privacy & Data

RiteHear uses platform data to operate broadcast, creator and business workflows. This page summarizes the main privacy position in plain language for listeners, creators and partners.

What We Collect

  • Basic account and submission data required to operate platform tools.
  • Usage activity needed to understand page performance and content demand.
  • Contact details when you submit inquiries, upload content or request partnerships.

How It Is Used

  • To manage creator review queues and programming decisions.
  • To improve scheduling, discoverability and publishing workflow quality.
  • To respond to requests, platform issues and business communications.
