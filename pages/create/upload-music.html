Submission Guidelines

  • File Quality Upload final audio/video files only, not rough references. We accept WAV, MP3, AAC, MP4, and MOV formats.
  • Complete Metadata Use complete information for title, artist/show name, and format. This ensures proper credits everywhere.
  • Broadcast Ready Programming review prioritizes broadcast-quality files. Clean audio, clear audio levels, minimal background noise.
  • Rights Clearance Confirm you own or have permission to distribute all content. We don't publish copyright-contested material.
  • Review Timeline Submissions reviewed within 24 hours. Approved content live within 48 hours across all platforms.
